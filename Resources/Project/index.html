Summary: Latitude vs. Various Weather Factors

What is the weather like as we approach the equator?

Latitude vs. Max Temperature

The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. To accomplish this analysis, we randomly selected latitude and longitude and then identified the nearest city. Then we pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API. We did this for at least 500 cities.

After assembling the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot various aspects of the weather vs. latitude. Factors we looked at included: temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity.
